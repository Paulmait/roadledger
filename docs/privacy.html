Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026 | Version 1.0.0

Your Privacy Matters: RoadLedger Pro is designed with privacy-first principles. Your financial data and location information are encrypted and never sold to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Company name, MC/DOT numbers (optional)
  • Financial Data: Trip records, expenses, income, receipts you upload
  • Documents: Photos of receipts and settlement statements for AI processing

Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates during active trips (only when tracking is enabled)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: Features used, screen views, crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: Track mileage by state, calculate profits, generate IFTA reports
  • AI Document Processing: Extract data from receipts and settlements using secure AI services
  • Sync & Backup: Securely store your data so it's accessible across devices
  • Improvements: Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the app
  • Support: Respond to your questions and troubleshoot issues

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase/AWS) in the United States
  • We implement industry-standard security practices including regular audits
  • Location data is only collected during active trip tracking

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share data in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Secure cloud hosting (Supabase), AI processing (OpenAI) - under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing (e.g., exporting reports)

5. Your Rights

All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in standard formats

California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

European Users (GDPR)

  • Right to access, rectify, and erase your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

6. Location Data

Location tracking is essential for automatic mileage calculation. Here's how we handle it:

  • Location is only collected when you start a trip
  • You can manually log trips without GPS tracking
  • Background location is used only during active trips
  • You can view and delete all location history in the app

7. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics may be retained for product improvement
  • Legal compliance data may be retained as required by law

8. Children's Privacy

RoadLedger Pro is intended for professional truck drivers and is not designed for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
